About

Courtney Lee is currently a researcher based in San Francisco. Prior to that, Courtney evaluated and analyzed program impacts at ScholarMatch, a college access and retention nonprofit in San Francisco. Courtney also worked as an independent data journalist at the Los Angeles Times, examining Los Angeles County’s efforts in bicycle infrastructure implementation.

Courtney holds a BS in mathematics with a minor in statistics from UCLA.
